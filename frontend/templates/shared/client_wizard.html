Credit Bureau Disclosure

The bank is required by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) to disclose the borrower's payment history, outstanding obligations, and general transaction behavior to licensed Credit Reference Bureaus.

Credit Check Authorisation

The borrower authorises Mainstreet Microfinance Bank to request credit scores, repayment histories, and default records from any credit bureau or banking institution in Nigeria to verify creditworthiness.

Cheque Recovery Authorisation

The borrower mandates the bank to fill and present any post-dated cheques deposited as collateral in the event of default on the agreed loan repayment dates.

This authorises the bank to fill and present your cheque as repayment if you default. Do not sign if you do not understand this clause.

Global Standing Instruction (GSI) Mandate

The borrower mandates Mainstreet Microfinance Bank to trigger a GSI command through NIBSS to debit any bank account linked to their BVN across all licensed commercial and microfinance institutions in Nigeria to offset default debts.

GSI allows the bank to recover outstanding principal and interest from your accounts across all financial institutions in Nigeria.
